Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the Lymphoma Classification System ("System"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and disclose your information when you use our System.

Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Health Information: Medical history, diagnostic results, treatment information, and other health-related data.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To classify lymphoma types and provide diagnostic support.
  • Communication: To contact you regarding your use of the System and respond to your inquiries.
  • Improvement: To enhance the functionality and user experience of the System.
  • Compliance: To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

Data Storage and Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted during transmission and storage.
  • Access Control: Access to your information is restricted to authorized personnel only.
  • Physical Security: Servers and data storage facilities are secured against unauthorized access.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating the System.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to your personal and health information.
  • Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your information, subject to legal and contractual obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt-out of receiving communications from us.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience on our System. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we will notify you of significant changes through email or a notice on our System.
